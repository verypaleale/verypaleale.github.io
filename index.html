About Me


Caitlin Huber graduated from the Universisty of South Alabama with a degree in Advertising and Communication as well as a minor in management. This was after completing her Associate's at Enterprise State Community College and a brief stint at the US Naval Academy. She prides herself on being exceptionally organized and working well with people from all walks of life.

Caitlin moved to Texas at the request of her good friend and former classmate with the intention of joining one of the many large tech companies that call Austin home. Frustrated by the opportunities available to her with her degree and experience, she decided to shift gears towards the future. Her roommate recommended the UT Austin Coding Bootcamp, and that's all she wrote.

In her spare time, Caitlin runs table-top games at her local comic shop and works on rehabilitating her adopted cat, Edison.
